About

Delight is a software development studio. Our goal is to bring delightful experiences to clients and users.

Our team has been working together for many years. In April of 2015, we adopted the name Delight and set up shop officially in Saratoga Springs, New York.

We are currently a team of 3 (and one puppy) with plans to grow. We are passionate about planning delightful user experiences, designing interfaces, and writing code.
