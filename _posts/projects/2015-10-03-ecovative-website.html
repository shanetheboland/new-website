Ecovative is a biomaterials company from Green Island, New York. They grow environmentally-friendly products using mushrooms. Yes, mushrooms.

We were hired to redesign Ecovative’s website. It was important that the new site had solid content and clear navigation.

The Project

Ecovative hired us to redesign their old website. The old website had confusing content and navigation. It was also difficult for Ecovative to make quick changes to the site. They needed a company that would listen to their needs, design a professional looking website, and maintain the website after it was finished.

It was important that the new website had:

  • A clear navigation and information structure
  • A fresh design with better typography
  • An admin panel where they could manage the website

The Client

Ecovative was founded by Eben Bayer and Gavin McIntyre during their time at RPI. Ecovative and Delight met years ago when we interned for them in high school.

Their innovative mushroom material can be made into packaging insulation, acoustics, furniture, buoys, wedding dresses, lamp shades, and many other things. They also sell a “Grow-It-Yourself” material you can use to grow anything you can imagine.
